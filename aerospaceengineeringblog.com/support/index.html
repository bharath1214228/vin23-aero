Support the Aerospace Engineering Blog

This website is a free educational resource and will always remain so. It is as much about me trying to pass on some of the things I have learned about aerospace engineering, as it is about me learning from interactions with readers (for a good example of this, see the discussion at the bottom of this post).

However, if you do feel like you want to support the website then there are many ways you can do so—from simply signing-up to the newsletter, to giving us a like on Facebook or leaving a review of the podcast on iTunes, or even buying me a coffee via a small donation.

That being said, the most personal way to support the website is to send me an email or to leave a comment at the bottom of a post about any questions you might be having. I love hearing from readers, and as I said above, I always come away having learned something through these interactions. So please do not hesitate to reach out: contact [at] aerospaceengineeringblog [dot] com.
